Strategic Goals

The key goal of the TTS is to enhance the economic prosperity of its members by carrying out commercialized farming, processing & managing market of organic orthodox tea and valuable medical herbs and aromatic plants (MAP) and other value-chain cash crops through this cooperative as an engine

Resource Mobilization

All the members will be encouraged and persuaded for investing resources (money, land, labour, Idea, Innovations etc.) as much as possible per the cooperative concept.

Accuracy and transparency of the records and accounts relating mobilization of investment, share collection, financial control, resource utilization, productive and outputs, inputs and income shall be maintained.

Joint investment and resource mobilization shall be carried out in coordination with the government, non-governmental, central cooperatives, foreign investors and organizations.

A Financial Guidelines shall be developed to adopt and implement the afore-mentioned approaches.

Institutional Arrangement

Based on the Statute of the cooperative, a series of Estate, Block and Unit shall be locally constituted in direct Central Committee or Board of Directors to be operational in direct supervision, guidance, direction, control and coordination as centre-local functional operation.

Leadership of the concerned local Estates and Blocks shall be vested to the active members of the Cooperative in the relevant locality.

The following Committees and Sub-Committees shall be established to take care and operationalise the concerned the concerned functional themes as per the Statue:

Think-tank Advisory

Farming and Production

Program Management

Institutional Growth

Quality and market Management

Accounts

Financial managements

Education and Training

Study and Research

Research, Innovation and Knowledge Management

Preference shall be given to scientific exploration, investigation and research by preserving, improving, modernizing and transforming traditional and useful knowledge, skill, art, culture and capacities, in context to designed agribusiness.

Highly technical and professional capabilities shall be developed utilize and employed to make the production, quality standard and marketing of organic orthodox tea, medicinal and aromatic plants (MAP), and other commercial cash crops having more market-worthiness & competitive at the international level

Property shall be preferred to find and achieve new and high valued products, species, varieties through the new research, study and scientific exploration of local indigenous varieties of MAP’s and Orthodox Tea of high mountain altitude.

Establishment of research, studies and development centers be carried on for Himalayan organic orthodox Tea (HOOT) and medicinal & aromatic plants & their products

Eco-Tourism

Arrangements shall be made for open, guided or sponsored trekking, observation tour or educational excursion of the farming plots of the Himalayan Organic Orthodox Tea (HOOT), medicinal and aromatic plants (MAP) and other cash crops plantations, Special beatutified area, Estates, Blocks nursery, and protected, preserved, developed and conservation areas.

Different agro-carnivals and events such as Tea Festival, farmer’s fete, cash crops crop gala, agro-tourism festival, products show, market exhibition, special product success demonstration and so on shall be organized.

Developing and prioritizing the identification of tourist destinations, cultural and entertainment centers, local arts and Agro-museums, indigenous handicrafts & amusement agro-parks, home-stay popularization, picnic spots, educational and information centers, high mountain heading centers, local traditional and herbal/medicinal markets, phyto treatment, traditional show and treatments centers, etc. based on indigenous specialities, culitre, history, religion, archeology, mythology, and tradition.
