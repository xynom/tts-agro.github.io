About TTS-Agro Cooperative Limited

TTS-Agro Cooperative Limited has been named Thamadada-Temke-Silichung Agrogocooperative representing the three mountains spreading from north to south in the mid-eastern part of the country, and it is anmed as TTS in short. The ultimate scope and goal of the organization is to develop, expand and manage the international market and trades dealing with orthodox tea, valuable medicinal aromatic plant (MAP) and other precious cash crops through local resource mobilization and indigeneous style of production. TTS has determined short, mid and long term goals and programs to extend strategic activities like commercial farming, producing and processing of organic orthodax tea and valuable medicinal herbsnd aromatic plants(MAP).

Programs

Conduction of different human resource development (HRD) programs such as training, orientation, informal schooling, seminar and workshops for enhancing capabilities of the cooperative members as well as institutional potentialities.

Insitutional Arrangement

Based on the statue of the cooperative, a series of Estate, Block and Unit shall be locally constituted in direct Central Committee or Board of Directors to be operational in direct supervision, guidance, direction, control and coordination as centre-local functional operation.

Resource mobilization

All the members will be encouraged and persuaded for investing resources (money, land, labour, Idea, Innovations etc.) as much as possible per the cooperative concept.
