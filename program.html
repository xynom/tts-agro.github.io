Program

Conduction of different human resource development (HRD) programs such as training, orientation, informal schooling, seminar and workshops for enhancing capabilities of the cooperative members as well as institutional potentialities.

Plans formulation and implementation of short term and long term (monthly, bi-annual, annual, five-yearly) action-plans for developing nursery, farms and production of organic orthodox tea, medicinal and aromatic plants (MAP)-like Chiraito, Taxus/ Himalayan Yew (Loth Salla), Rock foil (Pakhanved), cinnamum, Rubia, wintergreen, Malagiri, Halendo (wild turmeric), Satuwa etc. and many more other locally diverse species, and other cash crops like big cardamom, mushroom, beads (Rudraxha), Kiwi-fruit, Cirtrus fruits, Coffee, ginger, Titepati (Artemisin), Agarwood, Avocado, Timur, goodeberry, Asparagus, bamboos according to their ecological niche for commercial farming.

Acquisition and utilization of the local private or public barren, open and potential land through lease contract for durable period (50-100 years).
